About Me

A dedicated Bug Hunter with experience in penetration testing and ethical hacking

My journey in cybersecurity began in high school when I discovered my first vulnerability in a school website. This experience ignited a passion that has driven my career ever since.

Today, I focus on offensive security, bug bounty hunting, penetration testing, and developing custom exploitation tools using Python and Bash. My work has been recognized by numerous government agencies and private companies throughout Indonesia.

When I'm not conducting security assessments, I contribute to the cybersecurity community through research, developing custom security tools, and sharing knowledge with aspiring security professionals.
